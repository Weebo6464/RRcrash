    How this works
    
      
    
        We parse Pulsar crash dumps (.pul files) by scanning for registers,
        stack frames, and call chains. Instead of relying on external rules, the analyzer
        looks for signals like null return addresses, unresolved symbols,
        or suspicious register values, then produces a short explanation.
